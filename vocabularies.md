## 📝 **Usage Tips & Learning Strategy**

### **🎯 How to Use These Words Effectively:**

| Principle | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Context Matters** | Choose words that fit naturally in your sentence | Use "mitigate" for reducing problems, not "eliminate" |
| **Don't Overuse** | Variety is better than repetition | Mix simple and advanced vocabulary |
| **Practice Regularly** | Use these words in your writing practice | Write 3 sentences daily with new words |
| **Check Collocations** | Some words work better with certain prepositions | "contribute TO" not "contribute FOR" |
| **Start with Rank 1** | Master the basics before moving to advanced words | Foundation first, then build up |

### **📅 Learning Timeline:**

| Week | Focus | Goal | Daily Practice |
|------|-------|------|----------------|
| **Week 1-2** | Rank 1 words (core vocabulary) | Master 150+ foundation words | 10 new words/day |
| **Week 3-4** | Rank 2 words (advanced vocabulary) | Add 200+ advanced words | 15 new words/day |
| **Week 5-6** | Rank 3 words (academic highlights) | Incorporate 150+ academic words | 10 new words/day |
| **Ongoing** | Practice using all words in context | Maintain and improve | Review 20 words/day |

### **🎓 Study Methods:**

1. **Flashcard System:** Create digital flashcards with word, meaning, and example
2. **Sentence Creation:** Write 3 original sentences for each new word
3. **Essay Integration:** Use 5-10 new words in each practice essay
4. **Collocation Practice:** Learn common word partnerships
5. **Regular Review:** Revisit words weekly to maintain retention

### **💡 Pro Tips for IELTS Success:**
